Welcome to CROWN Dental Compliance

Crown Dental Is a specialist consultancy business designed to support dental practices to implement and maintain a maximum standard of care consistent with CQC regulations and GDC guidelines.
We ensure all your processes and procedures are managed efficiently to provide safe, effective, responsive, caring and well-led.

We offer
Updated policies and procedures 121 support
Health & Safety Assessments H.R. files for the dental team
Patient and Staff Journey GDPR policies and support
Mock CQC inspections Full support and assistance during inspections
Staff Compliance Training Regular visits to the dental practice
Evaluation of audits and risk assessments

As a team of specialists managers with an excitement and enthusiasm towards compliance, we strive to deliver the highest standard of service in a professional manner. We endeavor to encourage the dental team through training, supervision and team building. We are committed to providing excellent service dedicated to dental practices, helping them to achieve and maintain CQC requirements.

Dental Practice directors can rest assured that their dental practice compliance is in great hands!
