About us

My name is Iara Cabral and i am the lead consultant and founder of Crown Dental. Throughout my 10-year career within the dental field I have acquired a varied skill set which support my passion of compliance. Having worked as a Lead Compliance nurse and Practice Manager I recognise how busy the working environment can be and appreciate that changes to clinical governance are made often by our governing body.

My objective is not to sell to practices a new compliance system, but to use my GDC registration and knowledge to support dental practices and improve existing system.

All Practices value their performance and aim to deliver high quality dental care with sophisticated management. I help practices achieve these values and ensure ongoing quality assurance and compliance. All of these elements brought me to start Crown Dental Compliance and ever since I launched this project, the company has been thriving and has quickly gained a loyal following.

Services include a full practice assessment providing a report on gaps in compliance, reviewing policies, procedures, clinical governance, mentorship, coaching and personal development plan.
